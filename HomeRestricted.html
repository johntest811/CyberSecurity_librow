What is librow?

Librow is a library management system that helps libraries of all sizes automate their workflows and provide a better experience for their patrons. Librow is a cloud-based system, so it can be accessed from anywhere with an internet connection.

  • Solve and find solutions regarding the problem regarding the availability of certain books where some are having problems if the books are available within the library as some manually see if that certain book is available in their storage or shelves.
  • Provide an easy solution where the reader can browse and find the desired book that the reader wants to acquire or read.
  • Help people easily access our books where they can have a physical book or an Ebook copy where they can also have them physically or digitally borrow a book.

An online book lender and viewing project that serves as a central database for all of the books in stock, as well as their title, author, and price. The goal of this project is to create a website that serves as a central bookstore. The user can choose a book and check its price.
